Bánh Bò Nướng

pickyeaterblog.com1220min55min75min

This vibrant green honeycomb cake gets its color from Pandan Extract. This traditional Vietnamese cake is known as Bánh Bò Nướng. This cake has a spongy, soft texture, and lightly sweetened. The pandan leaf extract is nutty and pairs nicely with the rich coconut milk and coconut shavings on top!

  • 265 g tapioca flour
  • 35 g rice flour
  • 400 ml coconut milk
  • 260 g sugar
  • Pandan Extract to taste
  • 6 large eggs
  • 30 ml coconut oil veggy oil
  • 15 g baking powder
  • Butter to grease the cake pan
  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C and place the baking pan inside.
  2. Cook coconut milk with sugar until no longer grainy.
  3. Crack eggs and beat lightly with a fork. Do not overbeat otherwise your cake won’t have the beautiful honeycomb texture.
  4. Add in coconut milk and pandan extract and mix until combined.
  5. Mix all the dry ingredients together.
  6. Add the dry ingredients to the egg mixture and mix slowly until fully combined. Strain mixture through a strainer to prevent clumps. Add coconut oil.
  7. Grease the bundt tin with cooking spray and pour the prepared batter to the pan.
  8. Bake for 50-55 minutes. Turn off the oven but keep cake in the oven with the oven door partially open for 20 minutes. LET CAKE COOL DOWN UPSIDE DOWN!!!!
