Speca Me Gjize Ne Furres

410 min25 min35 min

Stuffed peppers from Gjirokastr, first tried when the grandma that owned our stay made it for us. Simple but scrumptious.

  • 4 peppers
  • 50-60 g ricotta cheese
  • 100 g grated kashkaval cheese or another semi-hard cheese
  • 3 tablespoons toasted breadcrumbs
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 garlic clove
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • bunch of chopped parsley small

Prepare the Peppers

  1. Wash the peppers and cut them in half. Remove the seeds and inner membranes.
  2. Pat them dry with a kitchen towel and set them aside.

Make the Filling

  1. In a deep bowl, mix the ricotta, eggs, grated cheese, and breadcrumbs until well combined.
  2. Add the chopped parsley and a little salt (be mindful, as both ricotta and kashkaval cheese already contain salt).
  3. Finely chop the garlic and mix it into the filling.

Assemble the Dish

  1. Fill each pepper half with the prepared mixture.
  2. Sprinkle some extra toasted breadcrumbs on top. Drizzle with a little olive oil.

Bake

  1. Preheat the oven to 180C (350F).
  2. Place the stuffed peppers in a medium-sized baking dish. Bake for about 25 minutes, or until the filling turns golden brown.
