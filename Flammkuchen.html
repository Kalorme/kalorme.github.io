Flammkuchen

Personal41 hrs20 min1h 20m

A german bacon pizza with cream instead of tomato sauce!

Dough

  • 275 g active sourdough starter
  • 120 ml warm water
  • 325 g flour
  • 5 g salt

Toppings:

  • High fat % diary product
  • Onions
  • Bell pepper
  • Mushrooms
  • Leek
  • Bacon Optional
  • Cheese
  1. Mix the water with the sourdough starter.
  2. Stir the salt into the flour. Then add the mixture and knead into a smooth dough ball.
  3. Place the dough in a bowl greased with sunflower oil and cover it with plastic. Let the dough rest for 10 minutes.
  4. Knead the dough briefly by hand (5-6 times) and then let rest again for 10 minutes. Repeat this step one more time.
  5. Let the dough rise at room temperature for 5-6 hours, until doubled in size. Or place the dough in the refrigerator at night and let it rise slowly. The next day, 1-2 hours before you want to bake the flammkuchen, remove the dough from the refrigerator and let it rise at room temperature for that time.
  6. Preheat the oven to the highest setting. Line a baking sheet with baking paper.
  7. Grease your work surface with sunflower oil. Divide the dough into two or three round dough balls (depending on how many pizzas you plan to make with it), sprinkle the dough with a little flour and then roll out each ball with a rolling pin into a round slice. Place the dough sheet on the baking sheet.
  8. Bake for 20 minutes

Instead of the sourdough, you can also use 60 ml water with 60 ml flour and 4 g yeast.
