Burger sauce

Personal5 min30 min30 min

Burger sauce based on In-N-Out but with small changes and adaptations

  • 4 shallots
  • 28 g unsalted butter
  • 2 dill pickles, finely diced
  • 180 g mayonnaise
  • 130 g ketchup
  • 30 g hoisin sauce
  • Salt, pepper and msg to taste
  1. Start by dicing the shallots and adding unsalted butter to a small saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add in the shallots once the butter is bubbling along with a pinch of sugar and salt.
  3. Cook over medium heat for 30 minutes.
  4. In a medium bowl, combine and whisk together mayo, ketchup, hoisin sauce, and a pinch of salt, pepper and msg.
  5. Add finely diced dill pickles and caramelized shallots. Mix together.
