Lentil Moussaka

choosingchia.com4-6 servings20min45min1h 5min

This Easy Vegan Moussaka With Lentils is packed with flavour and easy to assemble!

Vegetables

  • 2 medium eggplants, cut into 1 cm slices
  • 5 g salt
  • 2 large potatoes, peeled and cut into 1 cm slices
  • 25 ml olive oil

Ragu

  • 15 ml olive oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 4 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 g thyme
  • 2 g cinnamon
  • 400 g lentils, drained
  • 240 g crushed tomatoes
  • 30 g tomato paste
  • 80 ml vegetable broth
  • salt & pepper to taste

Bechamel

  • 30 g butter
  • 15 g flour
  • 300 ml milk
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • Cheese to taste

Roast the vegetables

  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C and line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Sprinkle salt on the eggplant and let it sit for 10 minutes, then pat dry with a paper towel. Toss the eggplant and potatoes with olive oil then spread evenly onto the baking sheet and bake for 15-20 minutes.

Cook the lentils:

  1. Heat the olive oil in a medium skillet. Add the onion and garlic and let cook for 2 minutes on medium heat. Next add the bay leaf, thyme and cinnamon, lentils, crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, vegetable broth, salt and pepper then lower the heat and let simmer for 10-15 minutes.

Bechamel:

  1. Add butter and flour to pan. Cook until brown. Add milk and stir to prevent lumps.
  2. Add pepper and salt and cheese to taste.

Assembly

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C.
  2. In a baking dish layer potatoes on the bottom, followed by eggplant and lentil ragu. Cover in béchamel sauce.
  3. Bake for 20-25 min

If you want to broil the Moussaka make sure to keep a very close eye on it so it doesn’t burn! It will only need 30-60 seconds on broil.

Store leftovers in an air-tight container in the fridge for up to 6 days.
