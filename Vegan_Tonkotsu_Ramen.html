Vegan Tonkotsu Ramen

wayoframen.com11h30h2h

Easy ramen with no meat

Veggie stock

  • 1,5 l Water
  • 15 g Konbu
  • 1 dried Shiitake
  • 1 Waxy Potato
  • 1 Onion
  • 1 Green Onion
  • 1 Garlic

Tonkotsu base

  • 300 ml Oat Milk
  • 100 ml Dashi Veggie Stock

Tare

  • 30 ml Vegan Shoyu Tare

Aroma oil

  • 20 ml Shallot Oil

Toppings

  • Roasted Tomatoes
  • Fried Shallots
  • Kikurage Wood Ear mushrooms
  • Green Onion
  • Spicy Bean sprouts

Make the Dashi Stock

  1. Soak 15g of Konbu, 1 dried shiitake mushroom in 1.5L of water overnight in a small pot.
  2. The next day, put the pot on the stove and bring the dashi up to 175 degrees Fahrenheit and hold at that temperature for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Remove the konbu and shiitake and reserve 200ml of dashi stock to make the vegan shoyu tare.

Make the Dashi Veggie Stock

  1. Add 1/4 of a waxy potato, 1/4 of a round onion, green onions and garlic to the remaining dashi stock. Simmer for 1 - 1.5 hours.
  2. Remove all the vegetables from the dashi and your dashi veggie stock is done

Make the Vegan Tonkotsu Base Soup (1 serving)

  1. Add 300ml of Oatmilk and 100ml of the dashi stock to a pot and heat up to 90Â°C

Bowl Assembly

  1. Add 30ml of the Vegan Shoyu Tare and 20ml of fried shallot oil to a heated bowl.
  2. Cook your noodles according to instructions
  3. While noodles are cooking add 400ml of hot Vegan Tonkotsu Base Soup to the bowl
  4. Strain noodles well of all excess water and add the noodles to the bowl. Recommended toppings are Roasted Tomatoes, Spicy Bean sprouts, Kikurage Wood ear mushrooms, Fried Shallots, Green Onions
