Chilli Con Carne

Personal420 min5 hrs6 hrs

This slow-cooked Chilli Con Carne might take a while, but guarantees perfect flavors.

  • 2 brown onions
  • 4 carrots
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 3 stick celery
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 8 g dried oregano
  • 8 g hot chilli powder
  • 8 g smoked paprika
  • 8 g garam masala
  • 4 g cinnamon
  • 500 g beef mince
  • 1 can tomato purée
  • 400 g chopped tomatoes
  • 1 beef stock cube
  • 500 ml pale ale
  • 1 can kidney beans optional
  • 10 ml Fish sauce

Optional

  • 25 g Dark chocolate
  • 4 cloves Dark garlic
  • 1 can Herb cream cheese
  • Fresh chili peppers
  1. Peel the onion, carrot and garlic. Cut them and the celery in cubes, mirepoix style.
  2. Pour the oil into a large oven-proof saucepan and add the chopped vegetables. Fry for a few minutes over a medium-high heat, until the onion begins to soften. Season with salt and pepper. Add chili’s if wanted.
  3. Add the oregano and dry spices then stir in the minced beef. Fry for 5 minutes to brown the beef then stir in the tomato purée and add the tomatoes. Cook for another 5 minutes then add the stock cube and the pale ale.
  4. Add the kidney beans and transfer to the oven with a lid once the mixture has returned to a simmer. Add optional chocolate and dark garlic. Finish by seasoning with salt and pepper and fish sauce.
