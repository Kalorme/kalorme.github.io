Karaage Japanese Fried Chicken

adamliaw.com41 hrs30 min24 hrs

Japanese ball shaped fried chicken.

  • 600g chicken thighs, skin-on
  • 15 ml light soy sauce
  • 15 ml sake
  • 3 g salt
  • 3 g pepper
  • 5 g sugar
  • 5 g grated ginger, juice only
  • 5 g sesame oil
  • 8 g grated garlic
  • 65 g potato flour or substitute cornflour
  • 65 g flour
  • 600 ml deep frying oil
  • Japanese mayonnaise, to serve
  • lemon slices, to serve
  • shichimi togarashi (Japanese seven-spice), to serve optional
  1. Cut the chicken into 5cm pieces. Combine the chicken with the soy sauce, sake, ginger juice and sugar and marinade in a ziplock bag overnight.
  2. Pour the flour into the ziplock and mix well. Shape the chicken in balls before frying.
  3. Heat the oil to 180°C in a wide saucepan. Add the chicken to the oil in batches. Deep fry for 2-3 minutes then remove the chicken to a rack and rest for a few minutes. Return the chicken back to the oil and fry until golden brown.
  4. Serve the chicken with a lemon wedge, and a little Japanese mayonnaise scattered with shichimi togarashi (if using).
