Fried Rice with Cabbage Slurry

25min30min30min

A fried rice version doused in a slurry cabbage mixture. From Takeshi Takeshima’s kitchen.

Rice

  • 200 g rice
  • 170 g Chinese cabbage
  • 100 g ground pork
  • Small knob of ginger
  • 1 egg
  • Salt to taste
  • A dash of pepper
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • Sesame oil to taste

Slurry

  • 200ml water
  • 2 g bouillon any
  • 15g soy sauce
  • 8g mirin sweet cooking rice wine

Fried rice

  1. Separate cabbage core from leaves and chop finely. Chop leaves in chunks.
  2. Cook egg first in wok until slightly runny, then set aside.
  3. Fry mince in pan until brown, then add ginger and cabbage core. Stir fry briefly.
  4. Add rice and toss until there are no more clumps. Add egg and soy sauce.
  5. Finish with sesame oil.

Slurry

  1. Add water, mirin, stock and soy sauce to a pot
  2. Add the cabbage leaves and bring to a boil
  3. Turn the heat down and thicken with potato starch mixture, while stirring.
  4. Remove from heat

Assembly

  1. Place fried rice in deep dish using upside down bowl trick.
  2. Pour slurry over rice
