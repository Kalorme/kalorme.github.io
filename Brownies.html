Brownies

joshuaweissman.com20 min40 min1 hr

(Very) fudgy brownies lekker yum

  • 170 g butter, melted
  • 282 g granulated sugar
  • 73 g flour
  • 80 g cocoa pow
  • 3 g salt
  • 2 g instant espresso
  • 2 eggs
  • 10 g vanilla extract
  • 113 g dark chocolate chopped
  • Top with flakey salt
  1. Whisk flour, cocoa powder, sea salt and espresso powder and set aside.
  2. In medium large bowl add sugar, melted unsalted butter, room temperature egg one at a time, and 10 g vanilla extract.
  3. Mix in flour mixture with the wet ingredients until nice, smooth and glossy.
  4. Fold in dark chocolate.
  5. Add mixture to greased parchment lined baking pan
  6. Bake at 175°C 35-40 minutes or until toothpick inserted comes out clean. Let cool to room temp before cutting.
  7. Use a metal pan over glass when baking to make cutting friendlier.
  8. Top with flakey salt.
