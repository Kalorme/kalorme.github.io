Animal Style Patty Melt

In-and-out style patty melt

Animal style onions:

  • 2 large onions
  • 25 ml oil
  • 40 g unsalted butter
  • 12 g salt

Animal-style sauce:

  • 177 ml mayo
  • 70 ml ketchup
  • 40 g chopped pickles

Beef patty:

  • 150 g minced meat
  • 1 slice Munster cheese
  • 2 slices American cheese
  • 2 slices White bread use milk bread it available
  1. Finely chop onions and cook in butter and oil until caramelized. Deglaze with vinegar if needed.
  2. Mix mayo, ketchup and chopped pickles for the sauce.
  3. Make smash burgers and put 2 slices of American cheese on the patty
  4. Sear bread in clarified butter and toast on other side.
  5. Smear sauce on bread and add slice of munster. Broil bread and patty until cheese melts.
  6. Add layer of onion and layer of sauce and chose the melt.
