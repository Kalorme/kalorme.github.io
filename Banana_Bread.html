Banana Bread

youtu.be430 min60 min12 hrs

Banana bread using yeast or sourdough, with an overnight fermentation.

  • 225 g unsalted butter
  • 375 g flour
  • 4 g yeast
  • 360 g sugar
  • 5 g baking powder
  • 195 ml warm water
  • 3 g salt
  • 4 g vanilla
  • 4 bananas or more
  • 2 eggs
  1. Combine butter and sugar in a bowl and beat them together until there is no loose sugar.
  2. Add vanilla extract and two eggs, one at a time. Continue mixing.
  3. Add the flour to the mixture.
  4. Mash the 4 bananas in a separate bowl and add them to the batter.
  5. Add the yeast or active sourdough starter, the baking powder and salt and mix until it is thoroughly combined.
  6. Cover with a damp towel and let it rise for 30 min to 12 hours.
  7. Preheat an oven to 180 ￼￼°C
  8. Butter the two loaf pans, place a piece of parchment paper on the bottom of the pans. Butter the top of the parchment paper as well and dust the pans with flour.
  9. Pour the batter in the two pans. Add toppings if needed.
  10. Bake for 45-60 min.

150 g sourdough starter can be used instead. In this case only use 300 g flour and 125 ml water.
