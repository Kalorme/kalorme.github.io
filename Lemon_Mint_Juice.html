Lemon Mint Juice

800 ml4 min1 min5 min

Refreshing summer drink from Egypt

  • 3.5 Lemons or 5 limes or 12 tiny limes. You can mix
  • 10 g Mint Leaves
  • 130 g Sugar
  • 700 ml Cold Water
  • 150 g Ice
  1. Cut rind/skin of lemon, mix all ingredients in a blender.
  2. Serve instantly or store in fridge.
