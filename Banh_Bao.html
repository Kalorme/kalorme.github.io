Bao bun yeast mixture:

  • 150 ml lukewarm milk
  • 12.5 g sugar
  • 4 g dry instant yeast

Bao bun dough:

  • 300 g cake flour
  • 60 g corn starch
  • 70 g sugar
  • 4 g baking powder
  • 1 eggwhite
  • 15 ml lemon juice
  • 28 ml oil
  • 1.5 g salt

Pork filling

  • 250 g minced meat
  • 140 g onions
  • 20 g spring onions
  • 15 ml soy sauce
  • 7.5 ml hoisin sauce
  • 15 ml sesame oil
  • 100 g vermicelli rice noodles
  • 100 g woodear mushrooms
  • 3 g pepper
  • 2 lap xuong sausages chorizo
  • 4 eggs or 8 quail eggs if possible

Beef filling

  • 250 g minced beef
  • 1 onion
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 150 g carrots
  • 5 g parsley
  • 7.5 ml hot chili sauce
  • 15 ml sesame oil
  • 4 eggs or 8 quail eggs if possible
  1. Make the baozi dough by combining all dry ingredients. Add yeast mixture and knead until a dough is formed. Add oil, knead until integrated and smooth. Let the dough rest under damp towel for 2-3 hours.
  2. Make the filling in a bowl by combining all ingredients other than the egg and sausage, mix until a paste is formed.
  3. Divide the dough and roll into flat circles.
  4. Fill the bao dough sheets with filling, egg, sausage. Close by pleading the dough.
  5. Steam for 15-20 minutes until inside is cooked through.
