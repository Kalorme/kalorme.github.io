Mizu Shingen Mochi

loveisinmytummy.com23 min7 min6h 10min

The Japanese Raindrop cake (Mizu Shingen Mochi) is a brilliant dessert to instantly wow your guests. 5 ingredients. Vegan. Gorgeous!

  • 450 ml water
  • 1 g agar agar powder
  • 1g sugar
  • 2-3 spoons of kuromitsu/ honey/peach syrup
  • 1 spoon of kinako/roasted peanut powder
  1. Combine the water, agar and sugar in a small saucepan. Stir to dissolve as much as possible.
  2. Bring to a rolling boil, and let it boil for a full minute as you stir continuously.
  3. You should see no cloudiness, no undissolved granules, just clear liquid.
  4. Let it cool completely.
  5. Pour into a semi-spherical mold. If using a spherical mold, do not close using the lid that came with the kit, rather just cover with cling film. We want a flat base for the sphere.
  6. Refrigerate overnight or for 5-6 hours at least.
  7. Run a small toothpick around the edge to loosen it up. Sit the mold in warm water for a few seconds to loosen the base.
  8. Unmold carefully and serve immediately with a side of Kuromitsu and Kinako.
