Khuushuur

kitchencoup.com1520 mins10 mins30 mins

These Mongolian Meat Pockets (Khuushuur) are eaten out of hand and are the perfect picnic or traveling food. They are tasty and filling.

Dough

  • 400 g flour
  • 532 g water

Filling

  • 500 g minced meat
  • 165 g onion minced
  • 5 g garlic minced
  • 5 g salt
  • 1 g pepper
  1. Mix all the ingredients except the flour in a bowl and set aside
  2. Add flour to another bowl and slowly add water while mixing the dough by hand until the dough is smooth and slightly sticky
  3. Remove dough from bowl and divide into four
  4. Take one piece of dough and cover the rest with plastic wrap so that it doesn't dry out
  5. Roll into a thick rope and cut and into 2 inch pieces
  6. Shape into a flat circle, stuff with tablespoon of meat and seal ends using a bit of water. See pictures above.
  7. Repeat until you use up the remaining ingredients
